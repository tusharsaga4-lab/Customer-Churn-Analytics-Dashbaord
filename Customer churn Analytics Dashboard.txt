# Customer Churn Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes telecom customer churn using Python and Power BI.

The goal is to identify high-risk customer segments and provide business recommendations to improve retention.

---

## Tools Used

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Power BI
- DAX
- Power Query

---

## Business Questions

- Which customers are most likely to churn?
- Which contract types have the highest churn?
- Which payment methods contribute most to churn?
- Which internet services are associated with churn?

---

## Key Findings

- Overall Churn Rate: 26.6%
- Fiber Optic customers have highest churn (41.9%)
- Electronic Check users have highest churn (45.3%)
- Most churn occurs within the first year
- Month-to-Month contracts contribute the highest churn

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Overview

KPI summary and churn trends.

### Customer Risk Analysis

Interactive analysis by service, tenure and payment method.

### AI Insights

Root-cause analysis using Power BI Decomposition Tree.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Promote annual contracts
- Improve Fiber Optic service quality
- Encourage auto-pay adoption
- Improve first-year customer retention
